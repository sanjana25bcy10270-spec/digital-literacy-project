STAY SAFE ONLINE – PREVENTION CHECKLIST

- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
- Always verify the sender before responding to emails
- Use strong and unique passwords
- Never share OTP or banking details with anyone
- Enable two-factor authentication where possible
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
- Regularly update your apps and devices

UPI Safety:
- Double-check UPI IDs before sending money
- Do not approve unknown payment requests

Report Cybercrime:
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Helpline Number: 1930
